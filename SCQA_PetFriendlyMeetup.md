# Pitch: Pet-Friendly Meetup Landing Page (SCQA)

## Situation
Pet owners are always on the lookout for welcoming spaces where they and their furry companions can socialize and explore together.  
Current meetup platforms focus broadly on events and locations, but lack a dedicated experience tailored to pet-friendly venues and gatherings. A simple, engaging landing page can serve as a proof-of-concept, gathering early interest and validating demand before investing in full platform development.

## Complication
Without a clear, attractive introduction to a pet-friendly meetup service, interested users may abandon the idea due to:
- Uncertainty about whether events truly welcome pets.  
- Lack of an intuitive way to stay informed about upcoming gatherings.  
- Difficulty visualizing what the platform offers and why it’s different from general meetup sites.

## Question
How can we quickly validate pet owners’ interest in a dedicated community for pet-friendly meetups and gather contact information for early adopters—without building a full backend or event database?

## Answer
Develop a simple prototype landing page with static HTML, CSS, and minimal JavaScript that:
1. **Captures Attention**  
   - Hero section with a friendly headline, supporting subtext, and pet-centric imagery.  
2. **Illustrates Value**  
   - Highlight 3–4 featured pet-friendly locations or upcoming micro-events in card format.  
3. **Gathers Leads**  
   - A concise sign-up form to collect email addresses and pet types, triggering an immediate UI response (alert or inline message).  
4. **Sets Tone**  
   - Mobile-responsive design, warm color palette, playful pet illustrations, and optional testimonial quotes.  

This landing page will:
- Load quickly in desktop and mobile browsers.  
- Visually communicate the concept of a pet-friendly meetup.  
- Provide a clear call-to-action for users to join a waiting list.  

**Next Steps**  
- Build the static prototype and share with a small focus group of pet owners.  
- Monitor engagement metrics (bounce rate, form submissions).  
- Use feedback and data to inform full platform development roadmap.
